Welcome to Atithi Devo Bhava!

We are based in the historical settlement of Anegundi, which dates back to the 8th century. Anegundi is located on the north shore of the majestic Tungabadra river directly across from the heart of the Hampi World Heritage Site

Our brand atithi devo bhava, has been named in homage to the village goddess and guardian Uramma.

Our vision is to restore and create beautiful heritage properties that are rooted in the unique architectural traditions of the region and yet provide a high standard of modern comfort for a global audience.These properties provide a unique combination of rural hospitality and modern amenities. Additionally, all our staff is sourced from the local community and they have been trained as per industry standards thus building their individual capacity and enhancing their abilities.

Our aim is to set a benchmark for hospitality and rural tourism in the Hampi World Heritage site through a scalable and replicable model with special emphasis on using local traditional building materials as well as local architectural typology. Eco friendly systems such as making all our properties a zero plastic zone, putting in place solid waste management systems and planting and maintaining trees have been put into practice.

Let us look after you and take back memories of an unforgettable experience!
